Conclusions

We present the first model that contains star formation, cooling, UV background, and AGN all at high resolution

Our model (zooms + ionizing MCRT + Lyα MCRT) reproduces observations of LABs

We produce LABs in all halos we model, suggesting Lyα is ubiquitous

LABs may be powered by emission from collisional excitations

AGN have a dramatic impact on powering LABs

The Lyα escape fraction from LABs is highly orientation-dependent, particularly in the presence of AGN
